Application Information

The information you enter for your character under the application is up to you: you can tell us as much or as little about your character as you'd like. You can even lie.

Think about your character profile as public knowledge about your character. If your character has secrets, you are allowed to protect them. But secrets can be hard to keep.

Powers

Here's where you'd put any public information about your character's powers, such as those granted by an ability or an artifact. You are allowed to "bluff" by listing powers your character doesn't have, or you can omit powers/weaknesses your character does have if you intend to keep it a secret.

Freeform

Please provide a minimum of 200 words but no more than 1,000 words. Include information about how your character died as well as what they have done in the year since their arrival in Home. You do not need to include "backstory" about your character's life before they came to Home. You can be truthful in your freeform, or treat this as your character's "cover story" -- the image they project to the public.

Plot Hooks

Please include three plot hooks that can start the plotting process! These can be relationships, plot events, etc., to help connect your character to other characters in Home.

The Truth

The Truth section should include the following: your character's gift (if it is not accurately reflected in your public profile), a full detailing of their powers/weaknesses (if applicable), and your character's cause of death (if different than that detailed in their freeform.) Anything else that isn't true can be revealed through plotting.
