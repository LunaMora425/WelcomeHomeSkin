The Commune

Population: 340

The compound of Saint Leyla and her adherents, The Commune is on the west side of Town. The Commune is responsible for nearly all of the agriculture in Home, so they have a tenuous relationship with the other residents of Town, who in turn provide many of the goods and services that the members of The Commune require.   • No Man's Land: the blocks immediately surrounding the Commune have been abandoned by Town so there is a dead zone/buffer between Commune and Town

The Farm

Run by The Commune, The Farm is to the west of Town. Here, there are fields, gardens, an orchard and plenty of pasture. The Farm has a coop of 60 egg-laying hens, 10 roosters, 12 dairy cows, 2 bulls, a flock of 20 sheep and 5 goats. The Farm was collectively managed by the people of the Town until St. Leyla and her cult instituted a hostile takeover of the Farm.
