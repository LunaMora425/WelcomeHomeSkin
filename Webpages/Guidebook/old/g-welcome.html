Welcome Home

Welcome Home is a paranormal horror roleplay set after a rapture event in which over a thousand people were transported to a mysterious town under the watching eye of an unknown being. The town is mysteriously blessed with basic technology, and the people have learned to provide for their collective needs. Some people have been granted supernatural powers; others experienced miracles such as healing of an illness, the return of their youth, or the understanding of a new language. But not one of them knows why they are here.

Certainly no one knows how to get out.

We are rated 3-3-3, meaning that we allow mature language, detailed sexual content, and explicit violence. We require all writers to be eighteen or older.
