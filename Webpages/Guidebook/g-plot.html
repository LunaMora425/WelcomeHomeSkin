Premise

On January 18, 2023, a series of disappearances occurred throughout the day and night. People who were supposed to die... didn't. In the moment just prior to their death -- in the moment before that rattly last breath in the hospital bed, before the car wrapped itself around the telephone pole, before the bullet tore through flesh and bone -- people vanished completely.

They woke up in a strange bed someplace else, a mock-up village set in a pastoral, temperate valley surrounded by mountains, a forest, and nothing else whatsoever. Everything was unoccupied and untouched, with no evidence of prior residence. There was no one but the newly arrived: no neighboring towns, and no government or infrastructure to be found.

However, there were homes: tidy rows of white picket fence homes dotting the hillsides. Inside, furniture and clothes and appliances were waiting. Those appliances? Running on electricity with no traceable source. The taps? Running water with no water tanks. There was everything anyone might need to start a new life, inexplicably provided, mysteriously functioning.

After the panic and dust settled, in the weeks that followed, the new residents exchanged tenuous explanations for their circumstances. Some have argued they are in purgatory, or that everyone is experiencing some sort of mass hallucination. Many have settled into the uneasy belief that they have been taken here by a supernatural force, be it a god, a demon, or something else entirely, and that it is watching them.

Even now. This very moment.

Trapped inside this idyllic sketch of a town, calling this new place Home, the residents have been given an opportunity to build a new society. Hopefully they'll put on a good show. After all, they are being watched.
