Town Locations

Town

Population: 998

The residents have built upon the skeleton of Town to form functional community, albeit one without some of the modern amenities residents had become accustomed to. Town sits in a valley with arable land and is surrounded on four sides by the mountainous woodlands known as The Blank. Town consists of two areas: The Residences, where residents live, and Main Street, a series of shops, governmental buildings and the Town Square.

Notable Main Street locations:

  • Town Square
  • Town Hall
  • The Clinic
  • The Morgue (new as of Aug 1, 2023)
  • The Distillery

Notable Residences locations:

  • The Hanging Gardens
  • Memorial Cemetery Park
  • Le Restaurant

The Commune

Population: 340

The compound of Saint Leyla and her adherents, The Commune is on the west side of Town. The Commune is responsible for nearly all of the agriculture in Home, so they have a tenuous relationship with the other residents of Town, who in turn provide many of the goods and services that the members of The Commune require. The Farm

Run by The Commune, The Farm is to the west of Town. Here, there are fields, gardens, an orchard and plenty of pasture. The Farm has a coop of 60 egg-laying hens, 10 roosters, 12 dairy cows, 2 bulls, a flock of 20 sheep and 5 goats. The Farm was collectively managed by the people of the Town until St. Leyla and her cult instituted a hostile takeover of the Farm.
