How to Apply

First Steps

  1. A Player (Out of Character) account is required before registering your first character.
  2. Because of how we handle moderation, announcements, and the help desk, joining the Site Discord is required for the duration of play on WHRP.
  3. Register your Player account with the Player Name that will match your Discord nickname. Please do not register with numbers or symbols.
  4. Please complete your Player Profile before registering your first character, found HERE.

Character Accounts

  1. WHRP is One Account Per Character. Register your Character accounts as First and Last name (unless not applicable) with proper capitalization, such as 'John Doe' or 'Jane Smith'.
  2. Character accounts can be registered from the "Manage Characters" section of the User CP, found HERE.
  3. Repeat first and last names are allowed.
  4. All characters in active play will be linked to the Player account. Any characters not linked to the Player account will be considered Inactive and archived.

GRAPHICS

  1. Character accounts must have an Avatar and Main Profile image. Signatures and other graphics are optional. Player Accounts do not need graphics, though you are welcome to have them.
  2. Avatar size is 200x300. Image will resize on a 2:3 aspect ratio scale. For an extra crisp image, use 400x600.
  3. Main Profile image size is 250 x 250 px.
  4. If you need help with graphics, please contact staff in the site Discord server for assistance.

Application FAQ

Do I have to use a faceclaim?

No. You can have a character description in lieu of a faceclaim in your shipper, and use “aesthetic” images or a moodboard in lieu of profile pictures.

Are there any rules about using face claims?

No faceclaims of anyone under 18. Don't use a faceclaim if you are aware they've asked for their likeness not to be used. Only actors, models and musicians are allowed. No YouTube personalities, influencers, etc. Do not use any civilian photos from social media.

Can I play a child character?

Yes. If you would like to play a child, please use character descriptions and a moodboard or aesthetic images.

Are there want ads?

Technically yes, but only if the characters died together. Anything else should be worked into a plot hook.

Application Information

The information you enter for your character under the application is up to you: you can tell us as much or as little about your character as you'd like. You can even lie.

Think about your character profile as public knowledge about your character. If your character has secrets, you are allowed to protect them. But secrets can be hard to keep.

Powers

Here's where you'd put any public information about your character's powers, such as those granted by an ability or an artifact. You are allowed to "bluff" by listing powers your character doesn't have, or you can omit powers/weaknesses your character does have if you intend to keep it a secret.

Freeform

Please provide a minimum of 200 words but no more than 1,000 words. Include information about how your character died as well as what they have done in the year since their arrival in Home. You do not need to include "backstory" about your character's life before they came to Home. You can be truthful in your freeform, or treat this as your character's "cover story" -- the image they project to the public.

Plot Hooks

Please include three plot hooks that can start the plotting process! These can be relationships, plot events, etc., to help connect your character to other characters in Home.

The Truth

The Truth section should include the following: your character's gift (if it is not accurately reflected in your public profile), a full detailing of their powers/weaknesses (if applicable), and your character's cause of death (if different than that detailed in their freeform.) Anything else that isn't true can be revealed through plotting.
