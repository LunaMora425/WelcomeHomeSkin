Time

For general purposes of consistency, the current time in Home is the current month minus 7. January 2024 would be June 2023, February 2024 would be July 2023, etc. We do not strictly police time in Home, but since there are items and powers that can be gained over the course of play, it is important for everyone to be roughly on the same page. Please keep track of your character's timeline accordingly.

Questions & Answers

Where are the residents of Home from?

Residents of Home can be from anywhere, so long as they were in North America at the time of their death.

What language(s) are spoken in Home?

English is the predominant language, although some residents do not have English as their first language. Most who couldn't speak English upon arrival have learned it through immersion.

What diseases exist in Home?

Currently, there are no known diseases beyond bacterial infections from improperly sterilized wounds, which is extremely dangerous in its own right.
