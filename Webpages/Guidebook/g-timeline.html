Site Timeline

The following is a rough guideline for how events have shaken out so far in Home, along with when characters actually arrived. Note this is sitewide; for individual character timelines please see the Dev Area. Got something to add? Drop it in #character-help in the discord.

January

Residents of the First Wave wake up in the morning of January 19th, 2023, in their beds in Home. Food, water, clothes, shelter, and heat are all provided for.

Arrivals

    February

    • Residents begin to explore Home in earnest. A street lamp is dug up to find the source of the electricity and there are no wires. When the street lamp is put back, it doesn't turn back on.
    • All but the most basic of staple foods stop appearing in the pantries. Residents panic as serious discussions around food production and starvation begin.
    • The Council is fully staffed by the end of February.

    Arrivals

      March

      • The Council solidifies the economic system and the rationing process in Home. Friction and unrest grows between Town and the growing faction under the direction of Saint Leyla, calling themselves the Commune.
      • The Hanging Gardens open the last week of March.

      Arrivals

        April

        The Commune has fully taken over The Farm within the first week of April, in time for the first spring planting.

                • The Morgue appears in Home.
                • A new area, the Memorial Cemetery Park, is created to bury the dead.
